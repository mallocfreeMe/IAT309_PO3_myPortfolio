For Businesses

Find Us On Yelp

Use the “Find Us On Yelp” logo to let your customers know you’re listed on Yelp, that you take pride in your reputation, and that you focus on the consumer experience.
For example, use the “Find Us On Yelp” logo in places such as your marketing collateral, website, storefront window, or company car or truck.
Please don’t alter or distort the “Find Us On Yelp” logo except to change its sizing while preserving the original proportions.
